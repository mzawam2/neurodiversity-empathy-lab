Reading Experience

Simulates aspects of Dyslexia A learning difference that can affect how someone reads and processes text

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. Reading can be challenging when letters seem to move or get mixed up. This simulation helps understand what some people experience while reading. Try focusing on each word individually as the text appears to shift and move, making it harder to maintain a steady reading flow.

Visual Experience

Simulates aspects of Visual Processing Differences Variations in how visual information is processed and perceived
Visual processing differences can affect how we see and interpret information on a page. About This Experience

This application provides simplified simulations to help build understanding of different cognitive experiences. Remember that these are approximations - every person's experience is unique and valid. The goal is to foster empathy and awareness of diverse cognitive styles.
